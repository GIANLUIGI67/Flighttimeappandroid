

If no braking:

LOSS OF BRAKING.

REVERSE .......... MAXIMUM.

If needed, the flight crew may keep maximum reverse thrust until full aircraft stop.

BRAKE PEDALS .......... RELEASE.

When the flight crew set the ANTI SKID & NOSE WHEEL STEERING switch to OFF, the alternate braking mode is activated.
Consequently the PILOT FLYING should release the brake pedals before the PILOT MONITORING selects the ANTI SKID OFF.
This action avoids brake locking.
Refer to DSC-32-30 Alternate Braking Without Antiskid.

ANTI SKID OFF .......... ORDER

The PILOT FLYING orders the PILOT MONITORING to set the ANTI SKID & NOSE WHEEL STEERING switch to OFF.
if no braking:

LOSS OF BRAKING

REVERSE....MAXIMUM

BRAKE PEDALS.... RELEASE

ANTI SKID OFF ....ORDER

ANTI SKID & NOSE WHEEL STEERING ....OFF

BRAKE PEDALS ....PRESS

MAXIMUM BRAKE PRESSURE ....1 thousand P.S.I.
Limit brake pressure to approximately 1 thousand P.S.I.. Monitor brake pressure on the BRAKES PRESSURE indicator.

If still no braking:

PARKING BRAKE ....USE

Use short successive parking brake applications to stop the aircraft.
For more information on the parking brake, Refer to DSC-32-30-10 Parking Brake.

ANNUNCIATIONS
Triggering conditions:
The T.CAS (in the SURV system) detects a Traffic Advisory (T.A.)

if the AUTO PILOT/FLIGHT DIRECTOR TCAS mode is available, the AUTO PILOT/FLIGHT DIRECTOR TCAS mode arms. For more information, Refer to DSC-22-FG-70-110-40 Introduction.

T.CAS mode.... CHECK ARMED(blue)
When the AUTO PILOT/FLIGHT DIRECTOR mode arms, the flight crew must be prepared to apply the AUTO PILOT/FLIGHT DIRECTOR T.CAS procedure in the case of a Resolution Advisory.
If the AUTO PILOT/FLIGHT DIRECTOR T.CAS mode does not arm, the flight crew must be prepared to apply the basic T.CAS procedure in the case of a Resolution Advisory.
If the AUTO THRUST is OFF:

AUTO THRUST....ON
The flight crew should set the AUTO THRUST ON to avoid the triggering of the AUTO FLIGHT AUTO THRUST LIMITED alert at th AUTO THRUST activation, in the case of a Resolution Advisory.

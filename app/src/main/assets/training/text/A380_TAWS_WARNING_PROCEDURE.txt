ANNUNCIATIONS

Triggering conditions:
The T.A.W.S. (in the SURV system) detects:
An excessive rate of descent at low altitude (PULL UP alert), or A terrain collision threat ahead of the aircraft (TERRAIN AHEAD, PULL UP alert). An obstacle collision threat ahead of the aircraft (OBSTACLE AHEAD, PULL UP alert).

For more information on the aural alerts, Refer to DSC-34-20-20-20 Aural alerts.

If TAWS triggers a PULL UP alert:
Simultaneously:
PULL UP TOGA

AUTO PILOT ....OFF
PITCH ....PULL UP
Pull up to full backstick and mantain in that position.
THRUST LEVERS ....TOGA
SPEED BRAKE LEVER ....CHECK RETRACTED
BANK ....WINGS LEVEL OR ADJUST
Aircraft obtain the best climb performance when the wings are as level as possible.The flight crew can adjust bank while climbing, provided that turning is the safest action.

DO NOT CHANGE CONFIGURATION (SLATS/FLAPS/GEAR) UNTIL CLEAR OF OBSTACLE

"TERRAIN TERRAIN" - "TOO LOW TERRAIN" - "TERRAIN AHEAD" - "CAUTION TERRAIN" - "OBSTACLE AHEAD" - "CAUTION OBSTACLE"

During night or I.M.C. conditions (simultaneously):

PULL UP TOGA

AUTO PILOT ....OFF
PITCH ....PULL UP
THRUST LEVERS ....TOGA
SPEED BRAKE LEVER ....CHECK RETRACTED
BANK ....WINGS LEVEL or ADJUST

Aircraft obtains the best climb performance when the wings are as level as possible.
The flight crew can adjust bank while climbing, provided that turning is the safest action.

DO NOT CHANGE CONFIGURATION (SLATS/FLAPS/GEAR) UNTIL CLEAR OF OBSTACLE.

During daylight and V.M.C. conditions, with terrain and obstacles clearly in sight:

FLIGHT PATH ....ADJUST
Adjust pitch, bank and thrust to silence the alert.

"SINK RATE"

Above 1000 feet A.A.L. I.M.C. or above 500 feet A.A.L. V.M.C.:
FLIGHT PATH ....ADJUST
Adjust pitch and thrust to silence the alert.

Below 1000 feet A.A.L. I.M.C. or below 500 feet A.A.L. V.M.C.:
GO‑AROUND ....CONSIDER

"DON'T SINK"

FLIGHT PATH ....ADJUST
Adjust pitch and thrust to silence the alert.

"TOO LOW GEAR" or "TOO LOW FLAPS"

GO‑AROUND ....PERFORM

"GLIDESLOPE"

Above 1000 feet A.A.L. I.M.C. or above 500 feet A.A.L. V.M.C.:
FLIGHT PATH ....ADJUST

When conditions require a deliberate approach below glideslope:
Glide Slope MODE ....OFF

Below 1000 feet A.A.L. I.M.C. or below 500 feet A.A.L. V.M.C.:
GO‑AROUND ....CONSIDER
